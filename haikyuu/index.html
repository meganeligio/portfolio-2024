context

the story takes place in Miyagi, Japan. the main protagonist is Hinata Shouyou, a first-year student at Karasuno High. he joins the boys’ volleyball team, and despite being only 163 cm, he aims to be the ace — the best offensive player — of the team. the story mainly focuses on Hinata’s volleyball journey and the people around him.
