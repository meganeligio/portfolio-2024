overview

As a student intern for The Board of Student Publications, I was tasked with creating a layout design for the Ka Hue Anahā magazine. It showcased short works written and submitted by students from Kapiʻolani Community College. I took the opportunity to refine my typography skills as I wanted each page to have a consistent look and easy readability.
