overview

Hello Stickers was a concept store that I made showcasing my own illustrations in cute sticker forms. I’ve always had a knack for adorable things and the idea of a fun sticker shop has continuously lingered in the back of my mind. I had yet to pursue it because it would take the time and effort I didn’t have. If I were to in the future, this would be my ideal design.

challenge

There were many competitors when it came to selling stickers, so I wanted to stand out from them. I wanted visuals that were pleasing to the eye and key features that would help the store gain exposure in a positive way.

solution

I designed an easy‐to‐navigate website that included reviews and a tracking visual for packages. By implanting these two features, it would promote the legitimacy of the store and secure the trust of potential customers. I used a dreamy aesthetic and scattered my stickers as decorations throughout the site in order to attract consumers.
