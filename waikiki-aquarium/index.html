gift coupon

I created a coupon that promoted an ongoing holiday sale with a shark being the focal point of the design. I printed out hundreds of copies, laminated them, and proceeded to cut out the bottom corner as indicated by the black outline shaped as a shark bite. I made two versions, one with a santa hat and the other one without.
